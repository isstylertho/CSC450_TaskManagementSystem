To add or update your calendar:

  1. Sign into your Google Calendar
  2. Hover over the desired calendar and click on the three vertical dots (More options). Select settings and sharing.
  3. Scroll down to the Access permissions for events section and check the box that says 'Make available to public'.
  4. Then scroll down to Integrate calendar section and copy the 'Public URL to this calendar'. Paste your link below.
